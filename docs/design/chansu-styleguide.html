Voice · 07 · build rule

Scientific register. No AI tells.

The copy discipline is the trust boundary made verbal. Terse, precise, provenance-hedged. The same rule that tags every claim keeps the prose unembellished.

No em dashes

Bufalin is potent — but toxic.
Bufalin is potent. Systemic toxicity limits use.

No AI phrasing

Seamlessly unlock robust, cutting-edge insights.
Matches four liabilities to precedent strategies.

Hedge by provenance

This modification improves solubility.
Predicted ΔtPSA -9.2. Cited precedent: fluticasone.

Do not over-explain

Here is a comprehensive overview of what this section will show you in detail.
C14 β-hydroxyl. High importance. Katz 2010.

Banned lexis: delve, seamless(ly), unlock, leverage, robust, cutting-edge, elevate, harness, testament, realm, landscape, "not only … but also", rule-of-three cadence. Prefer nouns with units and claims bound to a source.
